# Homepage Wireframe

## Goal of the page
Convert visitors into quote requests for home improvement services.

---

## HERO SECTION
Goal: immediate clarity + trust

Content:
- main headline (result-focused)
- subheadline (explanation)
- CTA button

Must answer:
"What does this company do for me?"

---

## TRUST SECTION
Goal: reduce doubt

Content:
- years of experience
- number of projects
- reliability signals

---

## SERVICES SECTION
Goal: explain offerings simply

Content:
- windows
- shutters
- renovation
- painting
- flooring

Each service = visual card + 1 sentence benefit

---

## PROCESS SECTION
Goal: reduce uncertainty

Steps:
1. contact
2. inspection
3. offer
4. execution
5. completion

---

## PROJECTS SECTION
Goal: proof

Content:
- real project images
- before/after
- short descriptions

---

## WHY US SECTION
Goal: differentiation

Content:
- why this company is better
- trust reasons

---

## CTA SECTION
Goal: action

Content:
- short reassurance
- CTA button
