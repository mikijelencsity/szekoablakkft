# Business Model

## Core business
The company provides on-site home improvement and construction-related services, focusing on residential and small commercial properties.

Primary services include:
- Window installation and replacement (core revenue driver)
- Shutter installation and automation
- General construction services
- Painting and finishing work
- Flooring installation (cold and warm coverings)

## Target market
Broad residential market:
- Homeowners
- Renovating families
- Small property developers
- Local businesses needing renovation or maintenance

The communication must remain understandable for non-technical customers.

## Primary value proposition
Fast, reliable, and high-quality execution of home improvement work with professional on-site service.

Key emotional promise:
"Your home becomes more modern, comfortable, and valuable without stress."

## Conversion goal
Primary conversion action:
- Request a free quote / on-site inspection

Secondary actions:
- Phone call
- Contact form submission

## Offer structure
- Free consultation or on-site assessment
- Transparent quotation after inspection
- No-obligation inquiry

## Business positioning
Mid-to-premium local service provider:
- Not cheap labor
- Not luxury construction
- Reliable, professional execution with premium perception

## Core differentiation (must be communicated visually and verbally)
- Experience and long-term presence in the industry
- Real project references
- Clean, professional execution
- Trustworthy on-site service

## Success metric
Website is successful if it:
- Generates consistent inbound quote requests
- Reduces hesitation before contact
- Positions the company as “safe choice”
