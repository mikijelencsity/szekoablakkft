# Homepage Visual Blueprint

## Core visual direction
Apple-inspired clean premium design with subtle blue accents, cinematic scroll flow, and strong photographic presence.

No neon. No heavy 3D. No clutter.

---

## HERO SECTION

Visual:
- full-width high-end real photo (house / window detail)
- slight dark overlay for text readability
- subtle glass blur behind text container

Layout:
- left aligned text block
- large headline
- CTA button below

Motion:
- slow fade-in on load (600–800ms)
- subtle parallax on background image (very light)

WOW element:
- text appears with soft stagger (line by line)

---

## TRUST SECTION

Visual:
- simple horizontal layout
- icons + numbers
- minimal background

Motion:
- counters animate on scroll (0 → value)
- fade + slide up

---

## SERVICES SECTION

Visual:
- card-based layout
- large imagery per service
- slight glass effect hover (subtle blur + shadow)

Layout:
- alternating left/right image alignment

Motion:
- cards reveal on scroll (clip-path or fade-up)
- hover: slight scale (1.02 max)

---

## PROCESS SECTION

Visual:
- vertical step line or horizontal timeline
- minimal icons

Motion:
- steps activate on scroll (highlight current step)
- line fills as user scrolls

---

## PROJECTS SECTION

Visual:
- large image grid (not uniform)
- mixed sizes (Awwwards style rhythm)

Motion:
- image reveal on scroll
- hover: slight zoom + info overlay

WOW element:
- before/after slider for selected projects

---

## WHY US SECTION

Visual:
- split layout (text + strong image)
- slightly darker background break section

Motion:
- text stagger reveal
- background slow parallax

---

## CTA SECTION

Visual:
- strong contrast background (dark break section allowed here)
- centered layout

Motion:
- button microinteraction (hover glow subtle blue)

---

## GLOBAL MOTION RULES

- nothing should move constantly
- motion only on scroll or interaction
- max 2–3 animations per viewport
- everything must feel slow, premium, controlled

---

## OVERALL FEEL

- Apple clarity
- cinematic pacing
- real-world trust
- minimal but not empty
- premium but not luxury showroom
