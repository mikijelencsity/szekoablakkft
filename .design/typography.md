# Typography

## Font
Primary: Satoshi

Fallback: system-ui

## Core principle
Typography is not decoration. It is the main interface.

## Headings
- Must feel authoritative and calm
- Avoid aggressive weight stacking
- Prefer size contrast over weight contrast

Rules:
- Max 2 font weights per page
- Avoid over-styling headlines
- Use letter spacing sparingly

## Hero typography
- Large, simple, emotionally readable
- Should communicate value, not service list
- Maximum 10–14 words per hero headline

Example:
GOOD:
"Windows that make your home feel complete"

BAD:
"High quality PVC and aluminium window installation services"

## Body text
- Max line length: 60–75 characters
- High readability over style
- Avoid dense paragraphs

## Hierarchy system
- H1: emotional statement
- H2: explanation / framing
- H3: structure / grouping
- Body: explanation
- Small text: metadata only

## Spacing rules
- Headings must breathe (large top and bottom spacing)
- Paragraphs must never feel stacked

## Tone alignment
Typography must feel:
- calm
- confident
- precise
- non-salesy in appearance, even when persuasive
